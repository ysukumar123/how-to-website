The Perfect Products
For the best oral care, use a high-quality toothbrush (preferably soft-bristled), fluoride toothpaste for enamel protection,

effective floss (either traditional or water flossers), an antibacterial mouthwash, and a tongue scraper to ensure fresh breath and healthy teeth and gums.
